KI-Checker

Flooded! museum exhibit

For my third-year design project, I worked in a group of six to design and build a museum exhibition about floods. I was in charge of visual design. More details coming soon!

Knit On/Off

For my thesis project, I'm making a mechanical knitting machine which will add stitches to one end of a piece of knitting and remove stitches from the other end. This work is a reflection on the way technology and craft intersect. This is a work in progress, and more details are coming soon!

I teach beginner knitting at Shall We Knit? in Waterloo, ON. I am available for private or group lessons, which can be booked by contacting me or through Shall We Knit? I'm happy to help you work through projects and I can teach these skills:

  • the knit and purl stitches (english and continental style)
  • casting on and binding off
  • fixing mistakes
  • what is gauge, why it's important, how to get it
  • increases and decreases
  • choosing and reading a pattern
  • various methods for working in the round
  • cables
  • lace
  • knitting with beads
  • interested in something else? just ask!

I am available to knit on commission; I can work from a pattern or design something for you. I charge an hourly fee plus materials. Contact me to get an estimation for your project.
